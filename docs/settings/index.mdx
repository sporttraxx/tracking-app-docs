# Settings

In the app settings you can customize your experience on Tracky.

<ScreenshotOSTabs page="settings" img="settings" width={300} />

## Race settings

At the top of the page under _RACE SETTINGS_, you find the settings that have been decided by the organization specifically for this event,
which you can not customize.

### Transmission interval

The time between location updates sent by your device.

## Other settings

### Measurement system

Changes the measurement system used to show your current speed. **Km/h** or **Mph** are available.

### Speed control

Enables speed limits signs and polygons if available.
