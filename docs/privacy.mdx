# Privacy Policy

Sporttraxx Tracky is a mobile application that enables race officials to track the location of competitors during a race. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use user data.

## Data Collection and Use

We collect certain information from users, such as location data and device status, when they use the app. For this purpose, this data may be collected even when the app is running in the background.

We use this data to provide location tracking services to race organizers during a race.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

By using Sporttraxx Tracky you consent to allowing information such as location data and device status to be sent to our servers, as well as allowing the data to be displayed on our webiste if the race you're partecipating in is available to the public.

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties for marketing purposes.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the app's data practices, please contact us at info@sporttraxx.com.
